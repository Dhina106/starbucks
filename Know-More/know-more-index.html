Try your favourite beverage in a Blonde Roast! It’s on us

What is the Blonde Roast?

Crafted from 100% Arabica beans, Starbucks Blonde Espresso is lightly roasted to highlight its mellow body, smooth finish, and delicate flavour profile.

What do we recommend to try with Blonde Roast?

Its lighter roast makes it an ideal base for milk-based and iced beverages, adding a subtle, balanced touch to classics like Cappuccinos, Americanos, Flat Whites, and Iced Lattes. It pairs exceptionally well with plant-based options like oat and almond milk, enhancing their natural sweetness for a smooth, rounded cup. Whether hot or iced, Blonde Roast brings flexible customisation to match evolving coffee preferences.

New Launches, Same Old Goodness!

  • Pecan Iced Shaken Espresso – A smooth double shot of Blonde Espresso shaken with ice and pecan-flavoured syrup, topped with creamy oat milk. Expect nutty pecan notes and a hint of cinnamon in every sip

  • Date Cortado – A warm blend of Blonde Ristretto, steamed milk, and date-flavoured sauce, finished with a dusting of date powder for a perfectly balanced, subtly sweet experience.
